UniSZA QS & THE Annual Preparation Timeline

Chronological phases for employer nominations, engagement, and survey campaigns

Phase 1 (July - Oct)

Industrial Database Mapping & Cleanse

Map existing and targeted MNCs to primary THE WUR subject areas. Validate contact directories.

Phase 2 (Nov - Jan)

MoU Formalization & Engagement

Convert targets to official MoUs. Bring corporate leaders into advisory boards or joint workshops.

Phase 3 (Jan - Feb)

Consent Acquisition Drive

CRITICAL STAGE

Reach out using UniSZA templates to request consent to nominate partners to QS/THE.

Phase 4 (Feb - March)

QS Partner Portal Submission

Compile contacts into Excel format required by QS Hub and upload before closures.

Phase 5 (April - June)

Follow-Up Coordination

Coordinate reminder emails asking contacts to check their inboxes for survey links.

English Template

Subject: Invitation to Be Nominated as UniSZA Global Industry Peer Representative

Dear [Partner's Name],

I hope this email finds you well.

As a highly valued industrial collaborator of Universiti Sultan Zainal Abidin (UniSZA), we are deeply appreciative of your continued support towards [Internship placements / Guest Lectures / Joint Advisory Boards] aligned with [Faculty Acronym, e.g., FPP].

In our commitment to benchmark our academic and graduate quality on a global scale, we would be deeply honored to nominate you as a Global Industry Representative for the upcoming QS World University Rankings Global Employer Survey.

If you consent, UniSZA will upload your professional contact information to the official QS Hub. QS will then send a brief survey link asking you to rate universities in your sector.

To confirm your consent, please click reply to this email with: "I consent to be nominated by UniSZA."

Thank you for partnering with UniSZA in shaping future global graduates.

Warm regards,
[Your Name]
Strategic Planning, UniSZA

Malay Template

Subjek: Pelawaan Pencalonan Rakan Industri UniSZA dalam Tinjauan Reputasi Majikan Global

Merujuk kepada perkara di atas, semoga Tuan/Puan berada dalam keadaan sihat sejahtera.

Pihak Universiti Sultan Zainal Abidin (UniSZA) ingin merakamkan ucapan setinggi-tinggi terima kasih atas jalinan kerjasama industri yang erat antara agensi Tuan/Puan dengan UniSZA khususnya dalam jalinan latihan industri, panel penasihat kurikulum dan pembangunan bakat pelajar bagi [Nama Fakulti].

Bagi memperkukuh pengiktirafan graduan UniSZA di persada global, pihak universiti berbesar hati untuk mencalonkan Tuan/Puan sebagai Penilai Majikan Global bagi Tinjauan Reputasi Majikan QS World University Rankings.

Pihak QS akan menghantar pautan maklum balas ringkas ke alamat e-mel korporat Tuan/Puan. Maklum balas daripada Tuan/Puan adalah sangat penting dalam menilai tahap kebolehpasaran graduan UniSZA.

Sekiranya bersetuju, mohon maklum balas kepada e-mel ini dengan kenyataan: "Saya bersetuju dicalonkan oleh UniSZA."

Sokongan berterusan Tuan/Puan amat kami hargai.

Sekian, terima kasih.
[Nama Anda]
Bahagian Perancangan Strategik, UniSZA
